解决go包管理代理网址无法访问：proxy.golang.org 默认使用的是proxy.golang.org，在国内无法访问 $ go install go: github.com/BurntSushi/locker@v0.0.0-20171006230638-a6e239ea1c69: Get “https://proxy.golang.org/github.com/%21burnt%21sushi/locker/@v/v0.0.0-20171006230638-a6e239ea1c69.mod": dial tcp 34.64.4.81:443: connectex: A connection attempt failed because the connected party did not properly respond after a period of time, or established connection failed because connected host has failed to respond. 解决方法： 换一个国内能访问的代理地址：https://goproxy.cn go env -w GOPROXY=https://goproxy.cn
